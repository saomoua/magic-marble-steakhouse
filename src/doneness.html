Beef Doneness

How do you want your steak cooked?

  • Rare:

    Seared around the edges, red interior. Should feel soft and spongy with slight resistance.

  • Medium Rare:

    Seared outside, pinkish red interior. Should feel fairly soft, fairly spongy and slightly springy.

  • Medium:

    Pink center surrounded by brown meat. Should feel fairly firm and springy.

  • Medium Well:

    Faint pink color throughout. Should feel firm with a slight spring.

  • Well Done:

    Solid brown color throughout. Should feel very firm and will spring back quickly.
